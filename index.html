About me

I am a third-year PhD student in physics at University of Texas at Austin. I am currently seeking a transition to computer science and will graduate with a M.S. degree in physics in Spring 2021. My interests of topics are reinforcement learning, lifelong learning and their applications in robotic. Started from July 2020, I was fortunate to work on some projects at the Learning Agent Research Group (LARG), which is directed by Prof. Peter Stone. (Find details of the projects here)

Research

My research interests include

  • Planning and control algorithms for robot manipulation

  • Lifelong Learning
